About Us

About Meen Samayal

Our commitment to quality and taste is unwavering, and we source the freshest and finest ingredients to create a meal that showcases the essence of coastal cuisine. The Meen Kolambu is a symphony of spices and fresh fish, simmered to perfection, while the Fish Fry boasts a crispy coating that gives way to succulent, tender fish inside.

Our Rasam is a harmonious blend of tangy and spicy flavors, and the sweet ending to your meal is a delightful surprise that will leave you craving for more.
